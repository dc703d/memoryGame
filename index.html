MATCH MASTER

  • Starting the Game:

    When the game begins, all cards will be displayed face down on the screen. Your goal is to find all the matching pairs of cards.

  • How to Play:

    Click to flip two cards. If they match, they stay face-up; if not, they flip back over. Keep flipping cards until all pairs are matched.

  • Winning the Game:

    The game is complete when all card pairs have been successfully matched. Challenge yourself to complete the game in the fewest number of moves possible!

  • Playing Again?

    Click Reset to shuffle and start a new game or click Return to Menu to exit to the main menu.
